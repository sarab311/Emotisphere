EmotiSphere

A spherical interface that translates input from physiological sensors into a musical composition.

Problem

There is an inherent relationship between emotion and music. People experience a range of emotions on a daily basis. Is there a way to utilize this connection in order to enrich emotions? In designing EmotiSphere, we attempted to address this question by connection the physical

Demo

EmotiSphere uses information from skin conductance and heart rate sensors to determine an emotional profile. Using that profile, a musical composition is created and played through speakers. The sphere lights up corresponding to the mood. Below is a video of a user scenario with a working prototype.

Design

Our initial design included an Arduino, two skin sensors, a heart rate monitor, and a base.

Our design included two main groups of interactions: on- and off-base.

Our current prototype uses an Arduino, two skin sensors, a PulseSensor, Pure Data, and has a 3D-printed stopper and base.
